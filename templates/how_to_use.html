How to Use

Follow these steps to use the Email Sender application:

  1. Enter the sender's email address and password.
  2. Enter the subject and body of the email.
  3. Upload an Excel file containing the email addresses of the recipients.
  4. Upload the resume file to be attached to the email.
  5. Click on the "Send Emails" button to send the emails.
